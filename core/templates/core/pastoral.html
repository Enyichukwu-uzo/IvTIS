Wellbeing

We believe that emotional health is as important as academic success. Our dedicated pastoral team provides counselling, mentoring, and a safe space for every student.

  • Full‑time school counsellor
  • Peer mentoring programme
  • Mindfulness and wellbeing workshops

Safeguarding

Ivory Tower is committed to the highest standards of child protection. All staff are trained in safeguarding, and we have robust policies in place.

  • Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL)
  • Regular staff training
  • Clear reporting procedures

Trips & Outdoor Education

Learning extends far beyond the classroom walls. We offer local day trips, residential adventures, and international exchanges.

  • Annual residential trip for Years 5‑6
  • Duke of Edinburgh’s Award programme
  • International exchanges with partner schools

Student Leadership & Voice

We empower students to take on responsibility and shape school life.

Student Council

Elected representatives from each class meet regularly with the Head of School to discuss student concerns and propose initiatives.

House Captains

Senior students lead their houses, organise inter-house events, and mentor younger pupils across all year groups.

School Prefects

Prefects serve as role models, assist with daily school operations, and represent the student body at official events.

Peer Mentors

Trained older students support younger peers with academics, social skills, and the transition to secondary school.
