Our Curriculum

At Ivory Tower, we follow an internationally recognised curriculum designed to inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning. From Early Years through to Senior School, our programmes are enriched with creativity, technology, and global perspectives.

  • Early Years: Play-based, holistic foundation
  • Primary: Core subjects + French, ICT, Arts
  • Middle School: Broad and balanced, preparing for IGCSE
  • Senior School: IGCSE and A-Level pathways
