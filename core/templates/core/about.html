A Message from the Head

Welcome to Ivory Tower International School — a place where every child is known, valued, and inspired to reach their fullest potential.

Since our founding in 2001, we have grown from a small community of 45 students into a thriving international school serving over 800 families. Our commitment remains unchanged: to provide an education that combines academic rigour with genuine pastoral care.

We believe that great schools are built on strong relationships. Our teachers know their students as individuals — their strengths, their challenges, their dreams. This personalised approach, combined with our internationally recognised curriculum, prepares our graduates not just for university, but for life.

— Dr. Amina Okafor, Head of School
